*Variance*
$$
\begin{align*}

Var[X] = 16 - 4^2 = 0 
\\\\

E[X^2] = 2^2 \cdot \frac{4}{6} + 8^2 \cdot \frac{2}{6} = \\\frac{16}{6} + \frac{128}{6} = 24 \\
Var[X] = 24 - 16 = 8 \\ 
\\\\
